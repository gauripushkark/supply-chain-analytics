# KPI Definitions

## 1. SLA Compliance %
Measures the percentage of processed shipments completed within the defined SLA threshold.

- Expedited processing SLA: 12 hours
- Standard processing SLA: 48 hours

## 2. Backlog Volume
Measures shipments that have arrived but have not yet been processed.

## 3. Processing Split
Shows the distribution of shipments across expedited, standard, and pending processing types.

## 4. Throughput
Measures completed shipment volume by facility and arrival date.

## 5. Facility Performance
Compares SLA compliance and processed shipment volume across facilities.
