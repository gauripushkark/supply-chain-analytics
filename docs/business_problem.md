# Business Problem

Supply chain operations often face challenges related to processing delays, backlog accumulation, and inefficient resource utilization across facilities.

This project simulates a supply chain analytics environment to monitor:

- Shipment processing timelines
- SLA (Service Level Agreement) compliance
- Backlog of unprocessed shipments
- Facility-level performance
- Processing efficiency across different shipment types

## Objective

The goal is to build an analytics solution that enables:

- Identification of SLA breaches
- Visibility into backlog and bottlenecks
- Comparison of facility performance
- Monitoring of throughput trends
- Data-driven operational decision-making

## Key Questions Answered

1. Which facilities are failing to meet SLA targets?
2. What is the current backlog volume and where is it concentrated?
3. How does processing performance vary by shipment type?
4. What are the throughput trends over time?
5. Where are operational inefficiencies occurring?

## Approach

- Simulated structured datasets representing shipment operations
- SQL-based KPI calculations and business logic
- Structured repository design for scalability
- Future integration with dashboards for visualization

## Expected Impact

This analytics solution can help operations teams:

- Improve SLA adherence
- Reduce backlog
- Optimize resource allocation
- Enhance overall supply chain efficiency
